Business Intelligence

A DPR (detailed project report) uses market research and a financial feasibility analysis to assess the viability and prospects of the planned facility. We assess current competitors as well as the demand-supply imbalance, determines the hospital conception needs, such as the bed mix, service mix, facility mix, or technology level. We develop detailed financial models (proformas) to evaluate the impact that different project design options, ownership models, offtake options, and finance structures have on project economics, with a focus on the clients operating expense and financial sustainability.

Hospital Planning & Design

The design of hospitals is complex as healthcare trends and demands are always changing. We emphasize patient-centred design principles, focusing on efficiency, patient flow, infection control, and user experience ensuring that hospitals are future-ready. From initial concept development to final construction documentation, we ensure that every aspect of the facility meets the highest standards of quality and safety. It includes:

  1. Detailed Master Plan:

    A comprehensive facility planning tool that takes future development into account as well as the number of blocks, support service and peripheral areas, landscaping, and internal road network regions.

  2. Space Programming:

    Aims to capture the scope and scale of the facility after the specialty mix, beds mix, and facility mix are finalized. It is a summary of department-specific areas, circular spaces, and the overall built-up area.

  3. Structural Design:

    The structural design of the entire complex entails developing detailed building plans as well as identifying the precise requirements of the desired area in harmony with the needs of the services and other project components.

  4. Architectural Design:

    Depicts floor plans of all areas of the hospital. It starts with defining operational flow, interdepartmental dependency, and relationships between functional areas. Based on that stacking & blocking and internal layouts are done.

  5. Engineering Services Design:

    Includes services like Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, Automated Vertical Transportation, Firefighting systems, Data and Voice Networking, Other Low Voltage systems, placement of biomedical equipment, servers and other networking infrastructure requirements, Building Management System.
